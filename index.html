How I Work

Modern Workflow

A structured, agile process — adaptable across 5-day sprints and multi-month cycles.

01
Discovery & Insights

User interviews, heuristic reviews, and problem framing — qualitative insight and quantitative analytics to define the right problem before designing a solution.

02
Ideation & Exploration

Sketching, concept generation, and rapid iteration — often facilitated through Design Sprints or collaborative workshops to generate and align on boldest solutions.

03
Design & Prototype

From wireframes to high-fidelity prototypes in Figma, Adobe XD, or Sketch — with WCAG 2.2 AA accessibility baked in from the start, not bolted on at the end.

04
Testing & Iteration

Rapid cycles with real users, iteration based on findings, and continuous feedback loops — validated against published feedback and behavioural analytics.

05
Implementation & Measurement

Working alongside development teams through delivery — writing specs, reviewing builds, and tracking measurable outcomes post-launch using analytics.
