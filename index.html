I'm a UX/UI and Product Designer with over 5 years of experience crafting digital products that are intuitive, accessible, and grounded in real user needs.

I've worked across public sector, SaaS, and mobile — collaborating in agile, multidisciplinary teams to ship meaningful products at pace. I'm comfortable owning the full design process: from early-stage discovery and user research through to detailed UI, design systems, and handoff.

I hold strong expertise in accessibility — designing and testing to WCAG 2.2 AA — and have deep familiarity with the GDS service design framework and GOV.UK Design System from working on public-facing government services.

Currently open to new opportunities across product design, UX, and service design roles.

Process

How I work

A research-led, iterative design process that keeps users and outcomes at the centre of every decision.

01
Discover

User interviews, stakeholder workshops, analytics review, and competitive analysis to frame the problem and uncover real user needs.

02
Define

Synthesis of research into personas, user journeys, and problem statements. Aligning teams around a clear design brief and measurable goals.

03
Design

Wireframes, flows, and high-fidelity prototypes in Figma. Iterating rapidly with cross-functional feedback, staying close to accessibility standards throughout.

04
Deliver

Usability testing, design QA, and detailed developer handoff. Measuring success post-launch and feeding learnings into the next sprint.
