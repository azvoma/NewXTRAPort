MindNest

The Problem

52% of users churned within the first 7 days, before experiencing the app's core value proposition. New users felt overwhelmed by options and unclear about how to build a consistent wellness routine.

Design Process

01Discovery & Insights

Analysed cohort data to identify churn patterns. Conducted diary studies with 8 users over 2 weeks. Found that users who set reminders had 3× better retention.

02Ideation & Exploration

Explored habit formation frameworks (BJ Fogg, Nir Eyal). Sketched 15 different onboarding approaches. Prioritised a progressive goal-setting model.

03Design & Prototype

Created personalised onboarding based on user goals. Designed a streak system and gentle reminders. Built an iOS prototype with micro-animations.

04Testing & Iteration

Ran beta test with 50 users over 2 weeks. Simplified goal-setting based on confusion feedback and produced a summary iteration report.
