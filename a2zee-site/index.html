About Me

Designing with purpose

I'm a UX/UI Product Designer with 5+ years of experience building scalable digital products that solve real user problems and drive business results — across GDS, SaaS, consumer mobile apps, public-sector services, and client projects.

I've worked end-to-end in product and design teams — applying GOV.UK Design System and GDS principles as a matter of practice, not compliance. I'm experienced working within GDS frameworks and have delivered in regulated environments where rigour and clarity are non-negotiable.

I believe great design lives at the intersection of craft and collaboration. Whether it's a 5-day sprint or a 12-week product overhaul, I bring strategic thinking, technical experience (HTML, CSS, JS), and close partnership with development teams to deliver outcomes that actually land.

I'm always keeping pace with emerging tools and methods — from AI-assisted design workflows to new accessibility standards — and I'm currently open to full-time and freelance opportunities.
