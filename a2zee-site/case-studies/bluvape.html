The Challenge

We're losing market share to competitors who better understand what customers want. Our brand feels outdated, and the experience is fragmented.

— Marketing Director, Blu Vape

Blu Vape was experiencing declining market share driven by increased competition, shifting customer expectations, and weakening brand loyalty. Existing experiences were heavily product-focused with limited emotional connection, long-term engagement, or community value. A 5-day design sprint was chosen to create alignment quickly, explore bold ideas safely, and validate direction before committing to further investment.

01Day 1: Understand

Facilitated strategic alignment sessions with C-level stakeholders. Led customer journey mapping identifying friction points and emotional drop-offs.

02Day 2: Diverge

Ran collaborative ideation using Crazy 8s. Each team member worked independently before sharing to avoid groupthink, then used thematic clustering to identify patterns.

03Day 3: Decide

Evaluated all concepts via dot voting and a decision matrix. The winning concept — an Integrated Loyalty Ecosystem — connected digital experiences with physical retail.

Tiered loyalty rewards
Curated starter kits
Community engagement
Tobacconist portal
Exclusive experiences
04Day 4: Prototype

Created mid-fidelity wireframes to validate information architecture, then high-fidelity prototypes reflecting the refreshed brand direction.

05Day 5: Test

Conducted moderated usability testing with 8 participants including existing customers, potential new users, and tobacconist representatives. Each session lasted approximately 45 minutes.
